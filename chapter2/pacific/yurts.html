The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a Yurt?
Our luxury are permanet structure four feet off the ground.Each yurt is fully enclosed with canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.
How are the yurts finished?
Each yurt is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove. Your luxury camping experince includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.
What should I bring?
Most guest pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with light layers al clothing. It's also helpful to bring a flashlight and sense of adventure!
